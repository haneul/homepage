Hello! I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science & Engineering at University of Washington. I am currently working with Prof. Thomas Anderson, Prof. David Wetherall and Prof. Arvind Krishnamurthy, and also closely collaborating with Dr. Matthai Philipose (MSR). Prior to joining UW, I worked as a software engineer at NHN Corp. for three and half years. Before then, I received my B.S. and M.S. degrees from KAIST.

I will be graduating in June 2016.
